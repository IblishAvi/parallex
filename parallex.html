BIKING

Mental health
Biking can help reduce stress and depression, and improve sleep quality. Being outdoors and breathing in fresh air can help you forget your troubles and enjoy the moment. Biking can also release endorphins that make you feel calm and relaxed, which can help you fall asleep and improve the quality of your sleep.

Weight management
Biking can help you lose weight and reduce body fat by increasing your calorie burn rate and improving your muscle mass. It's also a calming workout that allows you to ride fast or slow as needed, which can help prevent injuries to your muscles and joints.

SURFING

Physical benefits
Surfing is a full-body workout that engages your core, upper body, and leg muscles. It can improve your cardiovascular health by increasing your heart rate, especially when paddling. Surfing can also help you build strength and endurance, improve your balance and coordination, and increase your flexibility.

Mental health benefits
Surfing can help you clear your mind, reduce stress and anxiety, and boost your mood. The rhythm of the waves and the sound of the ocean can provide a sense of peace, while the physical activity and mental challenges can help you focus on the present. Surfing can also help you overcome loss and grief, and act as an emotional stabilizer.

Social benefits
Surfing can help you create a sense of community and belonging, and make new friends.
